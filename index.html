Features

Everything you need to automate your browser

🎬

Record Anything

Hit Record and use the web normally. MacroX captures every click, double-click, keystroke, scroll, input, and form change with millisecond-accurate timing.

▶️

Replay at Any Speed

Play back macros at 0.5×, 1×, 2× or faster. A live cursor dot traces every step so you can see exactly what's happening.

⌨️

Custom Hotkeys

Assign a keyboard shortcut to any macro. Trigger it instantly from any tab with one keystroke — no need to open the sidebar.

🔁

Loop Mode

Enable loop playback to repeat a macro indefinitely. Perfect for long-running automation tasks that need to run unattended.

🧭

Navigation-Safe

Macros that click links or submit forms continue playing on the new page automatically — your automation doesn't stop at page boundaries.

📋

Macro Library

All your macros in one place. Each shows duration, event count, and last-run date. Rename, re-hotkey, or delete any time.

How It Works

Up and running in 30 seconds

01

Install MacroX

Add the extension from the Chrome Web Store. No account needed — works instantly on any website.

02

Click Record

Open the MacroX sidebar, press Record, and perform the actions you want to automate. Press F8 or click Stop when done.

03

Name & Save

Give your macro a name and optional hotkey. MacroX saves it instantly to your local library.

04

Replay On Demand

Hit Play any time — or use your hotkey. MacroX replays every action exactly as recorded, at whatever speed you choose.
