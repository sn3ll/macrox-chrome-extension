Privacy Policy

Effective: May 3, 2026

MacroX ("we", "our", or "the extension") is a Chrome browser extension that records and replays user interactions on web pages. This policy explains what data the extension touches, where it goes, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

User Activity Data (stored locally only)

When you start a recording, MacroX captures the following user activity on the active browser tab:

  • Mouse clicks, double-clicks, and right-clicks (coordinates and target element)
  • Keyboard events (key names and modifier keys — not passwords; password fields are explicitly excluded)
  • Scroll positions
  • Text input values (excluding password fields)
  • Form change events (checkbox states, select values)
  • Mouse movement coordinates (throttled)
  • Clipboard text from copy/cut events

All of this data is stored exclusively in chrome.storage.local — on your own device. It is never transmitted to any server, never uploaded to the cloud, and never leaves your browser.

Extension Settings

MacroX stores your saved macros, hotkey assignments, playback preferences, and Pro license status in chrome.storage.local. This data lives only on your device.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information
  • We do not collect passwords or authentication credentials (password fields are skipped by the recorder)
  • We do not collect browsing history or visited URLs
  • We do not collect financial information
  • We do not use analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting services
  • We do not use cookies

3. Payment Processing

MacroX Pro upgrades are processed by ExtensionPay, which uses Stripe as its payment processor. When you purchase Pro:

  • Your payment information is handled entirely by Stripe — MacroX never sees or stores your card details
  • ExtensionPay stores only your payment status (paid / not paid) and the email address used for checkout
  • See ExtensionPay's Privacy Policy and Stripe's Privacy Policy for details

4. Host Permissions

MacroX requests access to <all_urls> so it can record and replay macros on any website the user chooses. The extension does not use this permission to read page content for any purpose other than recording the user's own interactions during an active, user-initiated recording session.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transfer your data to any third party. The only external service MacroX communicates with is ExtensionPay (solely to verify your Pro license status).

6. Data Retention and Deletion

All macro data is stored locally in your browser via chrome.storage.local. You can delete any macro at any time from the MacroX sidebar. Uninstalling the extension removes all locally stored data.

7. Children's Privacy

MacroX is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date above and (where possible) notify users via the Chrome Web Store update notes.

9. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Open an issue on our GitHub repository or contact us at the email listed on our Chrome Web Store page.
